CONFIDENTIAL (FR) 
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee to be held on Tuesday, May 3, 1960, at 10:00 a.m.  
1. Approval of the minutes of the meetings of the Federal Open Mar
ket Committee held on March 22 and April 12, 1960.  
2. Report of open market operations since the meeting of the Federal 
Open Market Committee on April 12, 1960, and action with respect 
to transactions during the period since similar action at the 
meeting on the same date.  
3. Review of economic and credit outlook.  
4. Discussion of: 
a. Open market operations.  
b. Other matters.  
5. a. Derivation of consensus.  
b. Record vote on policy indicated in consensus, 
c. General directive to be issued to the Federal Reserve Bank of 
New York covering transactions in the System Open Market 
Account. (See attached copy of directive issued at the meet
ing on April 12, 1960.) 
6. Review of the authorization to the Manager of the System Account 
at the April 12 meeting for the acquisition of one-year Treasury 
bills maturing July 15, 1960.  
7. Discussion of Treasury letter of April 13, 1960, regarding cash 
refunding. (See memoranda of April 21 and 26.) 
8. Date and time for next meeting of the Committee.
