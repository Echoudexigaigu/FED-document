CONFIDENTIAL (FR) 
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee to be held on Tuesday, May 24, x960, at 10:00. aim.  
1. Approval of the minutes of the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee held on May 3, 1960.  
2. Ratification of action taken by the members of the Committee in 
authorizing the sending by Mr. Hayes of a letter to Aubrey G.  
Lanston & Co. concerning the program of Government security 
market statistics.  
3. Report of open market operations since the meeting of the Federal 
Open Market Committee on April. 12, 1960, and action with respect 
to transactions during the period since similar action at the 
meeting on the same date.  
4. Review of economic and credit outlook.  
5. Discussion of: 
a. Open market operations.  
b. Other matters.  
6. a. Derivation of consensus.  
b. Record vote on policy indicated in consensus.  
c. General directive to be issued to the Federal Reserve Bank 
of New York covering transactions in the System Open Market 
Account. (See attached copy of directive issued at the 
meeting on May 3, 1960.) 
7. Review of the authorization, renewed at the May 3 meeting, for 
the acquisition of one-year Treasury bills maturing July 15, 1960.  
8. Date and time for next meeting of the Committee.
