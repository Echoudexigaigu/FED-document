CONFIDENTIAL (FR) 
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee to be held on Tuesday, March 27, 1962 at 10:00 aim.  
1. Approval of the minutes of the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee held on February 13, 1962.  
2. Report by the Manager of the System Open Market Account on open 
market operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee on March 6, 1962, and action with respect to transactions 
during such period.  
3. Review of economic and credit outlook, 
4. Discussion of open market operations and related matters; and 
derivation of consensus.  
5. Current economic policy directive to the Federal Reserve Bank of 
New York.  
6. Report by the Special Manager for foreign currency operations on 
those operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee on March 6, 1962, and action with respect to transactions 
during such period.  
7. Confirmation of date for next meeting of Committee. (April 17, 1962) 
For convenient reference there are attached: 
1. Current economic policy directive.  
2. Continuing authority directive.  
3. Continuing authority directive on 
System foreign currency operations.
