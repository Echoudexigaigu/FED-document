CONFIDENTIAL (FR) 
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee to be held on Tuesday, June 19, 1962, at 10:00 a.m.  
1. Approval of the minutes of the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee held on May 8, 1962.  
2, Report by the Manager of the System Open Market Account on open 
market operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee on May 29, 1962, and action with respect to transactions 
during such period.  
3. Review of economic and credit outlook. (Chart show) 
4. Discussion of open market operations and related matters; and 
derivation of consensus.  
5. Current economic policy directive to the Federal Reserve Bank 
of New York. (Copy of May 29, 1962 directive attached.) 
6. Report by the Special Manager for foreign currency operations on 
operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market Committee 
on May 29, 1962, and action with respect to transactions during 
such period.  
7. Confirmation of date for next meeting. (July 10, 1962)
