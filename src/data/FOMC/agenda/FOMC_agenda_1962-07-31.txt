CONFIDENTIAL (FR) 
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee to be held on Tuesday, July 319 1962, at 10:00 am.  
1, Approval of the minutes of the meetings of the Federal Open Market 
Committee held on June 21 and July 10, 1962.  
2. Report by the Manager of the System Open Market Account on open 
market operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee on July 10, 1962, and action with respect to trans
actions during such period.  
3. Review of economic and credit outlook, 
4. Discussion of open market operations and related matters; and 
derivation of consensus.  
5. Current economic policy directive to the Federal Reserve Bank of 
New York. (Copy of July 10, 1962, directive attached).  
6. Report by the Special Manager for foreign currency operations on 
operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market Committee 
on July 10, 1962, and action with respect to transactions during 
such period, 
7. Confirmation of date for next meeting. (August 21, 1962)
