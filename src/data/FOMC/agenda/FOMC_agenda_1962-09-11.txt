CONFIDENTIAL (FR) 
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee to be held on Tuesday4 September 11, 1962, at 10:00 a.m, 
1. Approval of the minutes of the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee held on August 21, 1962.  
2. Report by the Manager of the System Open Market Account on open 
market operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee on August 21, 1962,. and action with respect to trans
actions during such period.  
3. Review of economic and credit outlook.  
4. Discussion of open market operations and related matters; and 
derivation of consensus.  
5. Current economic policy directive to the Federal Reserve Bank of 
New York. (Copy of August 21, 1962, directive attached.) 
6. Report by the Special Manager for foreign currency operations on 
operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market Committee 
on August 21,,1962, and action with respect to transactions 
during such period.  
7. Further discussion of letter from Congressman Patman requesting 
permission to publish a confidential Committee Print of the 
Joint Economic Committee.  
8. Confirmation of date for next meeting.. (October 2, 1962)
