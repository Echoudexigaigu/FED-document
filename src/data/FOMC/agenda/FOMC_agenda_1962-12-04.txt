CONFIDENTIAL (FR) 
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee to be held on Tuesday, December 4, 1962, at 9:30 a.m.
1. Report by the Manager of the System Open Market Account on open 
market operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee on November 13, 1962, and action with respect to trans
actions during such period.  
2. Report by the Special Manager for foreign currency operations on 
operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market Committee 
on November 13, 1962, and action with respect to transactions 
during such period.  
3. Review of economic and credit outlook.  
4. Discussion of open market operations and related matters; and 
derivation of consensus.  
5. Current economic policy directive to the Federal Reserve Bank of 
New York. (Copy of November 13, 1962, directive attached.) 
6. Confirmation of date for next meeting. (December 18, 1962)
