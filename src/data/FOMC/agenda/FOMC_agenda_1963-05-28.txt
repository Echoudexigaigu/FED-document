dWFIDENTAL (OR) 
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee to be held on Tuesday, May 28, 1963, at 9:30 a.m.  
1. Approval of the minutes of the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee held on May 7, 1963.  
2. Reports of audit of the System Open Market Account and foreign 
currency transactions, made by the Board's Division of Examinations 
as at the close of business January 25, 1963, and submitted by 
Mr. Schaeffer, Chief Federal Reserve Examiner.  
3. Report by the Special Manager for foreign currency operations on 
such operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee on May 7, 1963; action with respect to transactions 
during such period; and consideration of recommendations of the 
Special Manager.  
4. Discussion of the proposal made in letter dated May 10, 1963, from 
Lord Cromer, Governor of the Bank of England.  
5. Report by the Manager of the System Open Market Account on open 
market operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee on May 7, 1963, and action with respect to transactions 
during such period.  
6. Review of economic and financial developments. (Chart Show) 
7. Discussion of factors bearing on monetary and credit policy; 
and derivation of consensus regarding open market operations 
in the forthcoming period.  
8. Current economic policy directive to the Federal Reserve Bank of 
New York. (Copy of May 7, 1963 directive attached.) 
9. Confirmation of date for next meeting, (June 18, 1963.)
