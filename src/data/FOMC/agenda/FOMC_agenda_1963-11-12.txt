CONFIDENTIAL (FR) 
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee to be held on Tuesday, November 12, 1963, at 9:30 a.m.  
1. Approval of the minutes of the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee held on October 1, 1963.  
2. Report by the Special Manager for foreign currency operations on 
such operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Marke4 Committee 
on October 22, 1963; action with respect to transactions duging such 
period; and consideration of recommendations of the Special Manager.  
3. Report by Manager of the System Open Market Account on open market 
operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market Committee 
on October 22, 1963; and action with respect to transactions during 
such period.  
4. Review of economic and financial developments.  
5. Discussion of factors bearing on monetary and credit policy; and 
derivation of consensus regarding open market operations in the 
forthcoming period.  
6. Current economic policy directive to the Federal Reserve Bank of 
New York. (Copy of October 22, 1963, directive attached.) 
7. Discussion of possible publication of Committee minutes for some 
past period.  
8. Confirmation of date for next meeting (December 3, 1963), and of 
tentative schedule of dates for meetings to the end of 1964.
