CONFIDENTAL (FR) 
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee to be held on Tuesday, December 17, 1963, at 9:30 a.m.  
1. Approval of the minutes of the meetings of the Federal Open Market 
Committee held on November 12 and November 26, 1963.  
2. Report by the Special Manager for foreign currency operations on 
such operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market Com
mittee on December 3, 1963; action with respect to transactions 
during such period; and consideration of recommendations of the 
Special Manager.  
3. Report by the Manager of the System Open Market Account on open 
market operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market 
Committee on December 3, 1963; and action with respect to trans
actions during such period.  
4. Review of economic and financial developments.  
5. Discussion of factors bearing on monetary and credit policy; and 
derivation of consensus regarding open market operations in the 
forthcoming period.  
6. Current economic policy directive to the Federal Reserve Bank of 
New York. (Copy of December 3, 1963, directive attached.) 
7. Confirmation of date for next meeting (January 7, 1964.) 
Attachments 
1. Current economic policy directive 
2. Continuing authority directive for transactions 
in foreign currencies.
