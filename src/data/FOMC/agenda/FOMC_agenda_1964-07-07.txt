CONFIDENTIAL (FR)
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee to be held on Tuesday, July 7, 1964, at 9:30 a.m.
1. Approval of the minutes of the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee held on June 17, 1964.
2. Report by the Special Manager for foreign currency operations on
such operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market Com-
mittee on June 17, 1964; action with respect to transactions during
such period; and consideration of recommendations of the Special
Manager.
3. Report by the Manager of the System Open Market Account on open
market operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee on June 17, 1964, and action with respect to trans-
actions during such period.
4. Review of economic and financial developments.
5. Discussion of factors bearing on monetary and credit policy; and
derivation of consensus regarding open market operations in the
forthcoming period.
6. Current economic policy directive to the Federal Reserve Bank of
New York. (Copy of June 17, 1964, directive attached)
7. Confirmation of date for next meeting. (July 28, 1964.)
