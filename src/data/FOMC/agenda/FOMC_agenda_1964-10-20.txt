CONFIDENTIAL (FR)
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee to be held on Tuesday, October 20, 1964, at 9:30 a.m.
1. Approval of the minutes of the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee held on September 29, 1964.
2. Report by the Special Manager for foreign currency operations on
such operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market Com-
mittee on September 29, 1964; action with respect to transactions
during such period; and consideration of recommendations of the
Special Manager.
3. Report by the Manager of the System Open Market Account on open
market operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee on September 29, 1964; and action with respect to trans-
actions during such period.
4. Review of economic and financial developments. (Chart Show)
5. Discussion of factors bearing on monetary and credit policy; and
derivation of consensus regarding open market operations in the
forthcoming period.
6. Current economic policy directive to the Federal Reserve Bank of
New York. (Copy of September 29, 1964, directive attached.)
7. Confirmation of date for next meeting (November 10, 1964).
8. Further discussion of the proposed change in form of the Committee's
current economic policy directive. (A discussion outline proposed
by Messrs. Ellis, Mitchell, and Swan, dated October 9, 1964, has
been distributed.)
