CONFIDENTIAL (FR)
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee to be held on Tuesday, November 23, 1965, at 9:30 a.m.
1. Approval of the minutes of the meetings of the Federal Open Market
Committee held on November 2 and 4, 1965.
2. Report by the Special Manager for foreign currency operations on
such operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market Com-
mittee on November 2, 1965; action with respect to transactions
during such period; and consideration of recommendations of the
Special Manager.
3. Report by the Manager of the System Open Market Account on open
market operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee on November 2, 1965; and action with respect to trans-
actions during such period.
4. Report on economic and financial developments. (Chart show).
5. Discussion of factors bearing on monetary and credit policy; and
derivation of consensus regarding open market operations in the
forthcoming period.
6. Confirmation of date for next meeting (December 14, 1965).
