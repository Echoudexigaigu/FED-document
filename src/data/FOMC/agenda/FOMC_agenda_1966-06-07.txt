CONFIDENTIAL (FR)
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee to be held on Tuesday June 7, 1966, at 9:30 a.m.
1. Approval of the minutes of the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee held on May 10, 1966.
2. Report by the Special Manager for foreign currency operations on
such operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market Com-
mittee on May 10, 1966; action with respect to transactions during
such period; and consideration of recommendations of the Special
Manager.
3. Discussion of memorandum from Mr. Coombs (to be distributed) con-
cerning draft Basle agreement on sterling balances.
4. Further discussion of proposed reorganization of instruments
governing System operations in foreign currencies. (See Secre-
tariat's memorandum dated April 28, 1966 and a memorandum to be
distributed suggesting certain revisions in light of discussion
at meeting of May 10 of changes proposed by Mr. Mitchell.)
5. Report by the Manager of the System Open Market Account on open
market operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee on May 10, 1966; and action with respect to transactions
during such period.
6. Reports on economic and financial developments.
7. Discussion of factors bearing on monetary and credit policy; and
derivation of consensus regarding open market operations in the
forthcoming period.
8. Current economic policy directive to the Federal Reserve Bank of
New York. (Copy of May 10, 1966, directive attached.)
9. Confirmation of date for next meeting (June 28, 1966).
