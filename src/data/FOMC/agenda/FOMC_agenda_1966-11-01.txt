CONFIDENTIAL (FR)
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee to be held on Tuesday, November 1, 1966, at 9:30 a.m.
1. Approval of the minutes of the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee held on October 4, 1966.
2. Report by the Special Manager for foreign currency operations on
such operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market Com-
mittee on October 4, 1966; action with respect to transactions
during such period; and consideration of recommendations of the
Special Manager.
3. Report by the Manager of the System Open Market Account on open
market operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee on October 4, 1966; and action with respect to trans-
actions during such period.
4. Discussion of staff memorandum dated October 3, 1966, regarding
System operations in agency issues.
5. Reports on economic and financial developments.
6. Discussion of factors bearing on monetary and credit policy, and
derivation of consensus regarding open market operations in the
forthcoming period.
7. Current economic policy directive to the Federal Reserve Bank
of New York. (Copy of October 4, 1966, directive attached.)
8. Confirmation of date for next meeting (November 22, 1966).
