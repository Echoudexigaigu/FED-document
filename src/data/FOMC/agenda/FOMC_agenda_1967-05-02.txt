CONFIDENTIAL (FR)
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee to be held on Tuesday, May 2, 1967, at 9:30 a.m.
1. Approval of the minutes of the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee held on April 4, 1967.
2. Report by the Special Manager for foreign currency operations on
such operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market Com-
mittee on April 4, 1967; action with respect to transactions dur-
ing such period; and consideration of recommendations of the Special
Manager.
3. Report by the Manager of the System Open Market Account on open
market operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee on April 4, 1967; and action with respect to transactions
during such period.
4. Reports on economic and financial developments.
5. Discussion of factors bearing on monetary and credit policy, and
derivation of consensus regarding open market operations in the
forthcoming period.
6. Current economic policy directive to the Federal Reserve Bank of
New York. (Copy of April 4, 1967, directive attached.)
7. Further discussion of the effect on Committee procedures of the
"Freedom of Information Act." (See forthcoming memorandum from
Mr. Hackley.)
8. Further discussion of Committee policy with respect to publication
of information on drawings under System swap network and on other
System foreign currency operations. (See forthcoming staff memo-
randum)
9. Confirmation of date for next meeting (May 23, 1967).
