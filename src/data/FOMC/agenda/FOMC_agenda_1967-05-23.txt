CONFIDENTIAL (FR)
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee to be held on Tuesday, May 23, 1967, at 9:30 a.m.
1. Approval of the minutes of the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee held on May 2, 1967.
2. Ratification of action of members of the Federal Open Market Com-
mittee on May 12, 1967, approving amendments of paragraphs 1A and
2 of authorization for System operations in foreign currencies to
include swap arrangements with the National Bank of Denmark, the
Bank of Norway, and the Bank of Mexico. (See attached copy of
authorization as amended.)
3. Report by the Special Manager for foreign currency operations on
such operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market Com-
mittee on May 2, 1967; action with respect to transactions during
such period; and consideration of recommendations of the Special
Manager.
4. Report by the Manager of the System Open Market Account on open
market operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee on May 2, 1967; and action with respect to transactions
during such period.
5. Reports on economic and financial developments.
6. Discussion of factors bearing on monetary and credit policy, and
derivation of consensus regarding open market operations in the
forthcoming period.
7. Current economic policy directive to the Federal Reserve Bank of
New York. (Copy of May 2, 1967, directive attached.)
8. Further discussion of the effect on Committee procedures of the
"Freedom of Information Act." (See memorandum from Mr. Hackley
dated May 16, 1967, and forthcoming memorandum from the staff
concerning pertinent records at the Federal Reserve Bank of New York.)
9. Further discussion of Committee policy with respect to publication of
information on drawings under System swap network and on other System
foreign currency operations.
10. Confirmation of date for next meeting (June 20, 1967).
