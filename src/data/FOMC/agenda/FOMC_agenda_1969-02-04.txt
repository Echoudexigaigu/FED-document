CONFIDENTIAL (FR)
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee to be held on Tuesday, February 4, 1969, at 9:30 a.m.
1. Approval of minutes of actions taken at the meeting of the
Federal Open Market Committee on January 14, 1969.
2. Acceptance of memorandum of discussion for the meeting of
the Federal Open Market Committee on January 14, 1969.
3. Report by the Special Manager for foreign currency operations
on such operations since the meeting of the Federal Open Mar-
ket Committee on January 14, 1969; action with respect to
transactions during such period; and consideration of recom-
mendations of the Special Manager.
4. Report by the Manager of the System Open Market Account on
open market operations since the meeting of the Federal Open
Market Committee on January 14, 1969; and action with respect
to transactions during such period.
5. Staff reports on economic and financial developments.
6. Discussion of factors bearing on monetary and credit policy;
and derivation of consensus regarding open market operations
in the forthcoming period.
7. Current economic policy directive to the Federal Reserve Bank
of New York. (Copy of January 14, 1969, directive attached.)
8. Discussion of problems relating to Treasury cash position and
debt ceiling. (See forthcoming memorandum from Manager.)
9. Confirmation of date for next meeting (March 4, 1969).
