Committee to be held on Tuesday, June 8, 1971, at 9:30 a.m.

Open Market Committee on May 11, 1971.

Federal Open Market Committee on May 11, 1971.

A. Report by the Special Manager on foreign currency operations
since the meeting of the Committee on May 11, 1971; and action
with respect to transactions during that period.
B. Other reports on foreign meetings.
C. Staff report on international developments.
D. Consideration of recommendations of Special Manager.
4. Economic and financial situation and outlook.
A. Staff report.
B. General discussion.
5. Domestic open market operations.
A. Report by Manager on open market operations since the meeting
of the Committee on May 11, 1971; and action with respect to
transactions during that period.
B. Staff comments on the monetary relationships described in
blue book.
