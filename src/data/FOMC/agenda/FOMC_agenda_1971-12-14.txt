CONFIDENTIAL (FR)
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee to be held on Tuesday, December 14, 1971, at 9:30 a.m.
1. Approval of minutes of actions taken at the meeting of the
Federal Open Market Committee on November 16, 1971.
2. Acceptance of memorandum of discussion for the meeting of the
Federal Open Market Committee on November 16, 1971.
3. Foreign currency operations.
A. Reports on foreign meetings.
B. Report by Special Manager on foreign currency operations
since the meeting of the Committee on November 16, 1971;
and action with respect to transactions during that period.
C. Consideration of recommendations of Special Manager.
4. Economic and financial situation and outlook.
A. Staff report.
B. General Committee discussion.
5. Domestic open market operations.
A. Report by Manager on open market operations since the meeting
of the Committee on November 16, 1971; and action with respect
to transactions during that period.
B. Staff comments on monetary relationships described in
blue book.

6, Discussion of monetary policy and of current economic policy
directive to be issued to the Federal Reserve Bank of New
York. (Copy of November 16, 1971, directive attached.)
7. Discussion of release of 1966 Federal Open Market Committee
minutes. (See forthcoming memorandum from Secretariat.)
8. Confirmation of date for next meeting (January 18, 1972).
