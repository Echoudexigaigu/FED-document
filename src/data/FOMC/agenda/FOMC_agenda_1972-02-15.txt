CONFIDENTIAL (FR)
Agendas for Meetings to be Held on
Monday, February 14, 1972
Meeting of the Federal Open Market Committee (2:30 p.m.)
1. Discussion of operating targets. (See "Third Report
of the Committee on the Directive," and memorandum
from the System Account Manager entitled "Reserve
targets," both distributed on January 19, 1972.)
Joint Meeting of the Board of Governors and Reserve Bank Presidents
(to follow FOMC meeting)
1. Discussion of reserve requirements.
2. (If time permits) Discussion of discount rate-setting
procedures.

CONFIDENTIAL (FR)
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee to be held on Tuesday, February 15, 1972, at 9:30 a.m.
1. Ratification of action of Committee members on January 26, 1972,
suspending until close of business on February 15, 1972, the
lower limit on interest rate on repurchase agreements specified
in paragraph 1(c) of the continuing authority directive.
2. Approval of minutes of actions taken at the meeting of the
Federal Open Market Committee on December 14, 1971.
3. Acceptance of memorandum of discussion for the meeting of the
Federal Open Market Committee on December 14, 1971.
4. Foreign currency operations.
A. Reports on foreign meetings.
B. Report by Special Manager on foreign currency operations
since the meeting of the Committee on January 11, 1972;
and action with respect to transactions during that period.
C. Consideration of recommendations of Special Manager.
5. Economic and financial situation and outlook.
A. Staff report.
B. General Committee discussion.
6. Domestic open market operations.
A. Report by Manager on open market operations since the meeting
of the Committee on January 11, 1972; and action with respect
to transactions during that period.
B. Staff comments on monetary relationships described in
blue book.

7. Discussion of monetary policy and of current economic policy
directive to be issued to the Federal Reserve Bank of New
York. (Copy of January 11, 1972, directive attached.)
8. Proposed amendment to guidelines for outright operations in
Federal agency issues. (See memorandum from Manager dated
January 25, 1972, and entitled "Transactions in Government
Agency Issues.")
9. Confirmation of date for next meeting (March 21, 1972).
