CONFIDENTIAL (FR)
Matters for consideration at the meeting of th Federal Open Market
Committee to be held on Tuesday, September 19, 1972 at 9:30 a.m.
1. Approval of minutes of actions taken at the meetings of the
Federal Open Market Committee on July 18, 1972.
2. Acceptance of memoranda of discussion for the meetings of the
Federal Open Market Committee on July 18, 1972.
3. Foreign currency operations.
A. Reports on foreign meetings.
B. Report by Special Manager on foreign currency operations
since the meeting of the Committee on August 15, 1972; and
action with respect to transactions during that period.
C. Consideration of recommendations of Special Manager.
4. Economic and financial situation and outlook.
A. Staff report.
B. General Committee discussion.
5. Domestic open market operations.
A. Report by Manager on open market operations since the meeting
of the Committee on August 15, 1972; and action with respect
to transactions during that period.
B. Comments by Economist (Domestic Finance) on prospective
financial relationships.

6. Discussion of monetary policy and of current economic policy
directive to be issued to the Federal Reserve Bank of New York.
(Copy of August 15, 1972, directive attached.)
7. Consideration of 1973 Committee meeting schedule. (See memorandum
from Secretariat dated September 12, 1972.)
8. Confirmation of date for next meeting (October 17, 1972).
