CONFIDENTIAL (FR)
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee to be held on Tuesday, December 19, 1972, at 9:30 a.m.
1. Foreign currency operations.
A. Reports on foreign meetings.
B. Report by Special Manager on foreign currency operations
since the meeting of the Committee on November 21, 1972;
and action with respect to transactions during that period.
C. Consideration of recommendations of Special Manager.
2. Economic and financial situation and outlook.
A. Staff report.
B. General Committee discussion.
3. Domestic open market operations.
A. Report by Manager on open market operations since the meeting
of the Committee on November 21, 1972; and action with respect
to transactions during that period.
B. Comments by Economist (Domestic Finance) on prospective
financial relationships.
4. Discussion of monetary policy and of current economic policy
directive to be issued to the Federal Reserve Bank of New York.
(Copy of November 21, 1972, directive attached.)
5. Confirmation of date for next meeting (January 16, 1973).
