CONFIDENTIAL (FR)
Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee to be held on Wednesday, September 11, 1974, at 9:30 a.m.
1. Foreign currency operations.
A. Report by Special Manager on foreign currency operations
since the meeting of the Committee on August 20, 1974;
and action with respect to transactions during that period.
B. Consideration of recommendations of Special Manager.
2. Economic and financial situation and outlook.
A. Comments by Staff.
B. General Committee discussion.
3. Domestic open market operations.
A. Report by Manager on open market operations since the
meeting of the Committee on August 20, 1974; and
action with respect to transactions during that period.
B. Comments by Economist (Domestic Finance) on prospective
financial relationships.

4. Limited session: Discussion of monetary policy (including
longer-run targets) and of domestic policy directive to be
issued to the Federal Reserve Bank of New York.
(Copy of August 20, 1974, directive attached.)
5. Confirmation of date for next meeting (October 15, 1974).
