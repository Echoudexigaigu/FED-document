Matters for consideration at the meeting of the Federal Open Market
Committee to be held on Monday and Tuesday, January 20 and 21, 1975
Monday, 4:00 p.m.
1. Discussion of first-stage report of the Subcommittee on the
Directive (distributed on December 24, 1974).
Tuesday, 9:30 a.m.
2. Approval of minutes of actions taken at the meeting of the
Federal Open Market Committee on December 16-17, 1974.
3. Acceptance of memorandum of discussion for the meeting of
the Federal Open Market Committee on December 16-17, 1974.

A. Report by Special Manager on foreign currency operations
since the meeting of the Committee on December 17, 1974;
and action with respect to transactions during that period.

5. Economic and financial situation and outlook.
A. Comments by Senior Economist.
B. General Committee discussion.

financial relationships.
